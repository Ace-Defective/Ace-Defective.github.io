Hello World! My name is Aidan Dugan and I am currently a Security Specialist graduating with a degree in Cybersecurity Analytics and Operations with a minor in Security Risk Analysis from Penn State University (Hooray!)

I was inspired to go into information security during my senior year of high school when I joined a pilot class for a program called "NuPaths". It was promised to be a dual enrollment opportunity to gain a number of college credits for careers within IT. I thought nothing of it at the time, but that class changed my life and set me on the path I am on today. I was inspired by the hands on knowledge I had received through the program and my professors encouraged me to pursue Cybersecurity further in my academic career going into college.

I worked hard as a student working part time while maintaining my place within the Honors college at Penn State Harrisburg. During the summer of 2024 I interned at Select Medical, a fortune 500 company specializing in post acute healthcare. I worked within their Identity and Access Management department as an automation developer creating software robots for the automated provisioning of users. This summer project was then presented to a board of constituents including my peers, my mentors, and the C-level executives at the company. The company was so impressed with their work they offered to extend part time work to me so that I may continue my development of my automations. Currently the sum of my automations have provisioned access for thousands of users and have been estimated to free up over 600 FTE hours each year.

Going forward, I hope to continue my professional development through education. I'm currently pursuing my CompTIA Security+ certificaiton as well as my SC-200 certificaiton to become more familiar with the Microsoft Security suite of applications.
